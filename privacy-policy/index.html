Our Commitment to Your Privacy
We are proud to be a leader in the field of electronic commerce and a true innovator in the financial services industry. We value your trust in us and will work hard to earn your confidence so that you can enthusiastically use our services and recommend us to others.

Information We Collect
We collect personally identifiable information (“Personal Data”) about you that you specifically provide to us while using the Website. Personal Data includes information that can identify you as a specific individual, such as your name, address, phone number or e-mail address and IP address.

As part of our evaluation and monitoring process, we may also collect Personal Data from non-affiliated third parties such as credit bureaus, employers, and financial institutions.

Protecting Your Personal Data
We take our responsibility to protect the privacy and confidentiality of Personal Data very seriously. We maintain physical, electronic, and procedural safeguards that are designed to comply with federal standards to store and secure your information from unauthorized access, alteration, and destruction. Our control policies restrict access to your personal and account information to those employees who need to access that information to provide products or services to you.

The Personal Data you provide us is saved in a secure environment and is kept in our records to make it easier for you to use our services on return visits to our Website.

To prevent unauthorized access, maintain data accuracy, and ensure the correct use of information, we will employ reasonable and current Internet security methods and technologies. We currently use Secure Socket Layer (SSL) technology to encrypt your Personal Data to prevent your information from being read during transmission over the Internet.

How We Use the Information We Collect
We use the Personal Data we collect to provide you with the products and services you use through our programs.

Disclosure to Third Parties
We share Personal Data with non-affiliated third parties that provide services to us or with you for the operation of our programs, including banks that operate as the depository/ institution for products we offer. In other cases, we only disclose Personal Data to non-affiliated third parties as permitted or required by law.

Our Policy Concerning Cookies
To serve you faster and with better quality, we use “cookies” technology. Cookies are small bits of code, usually stored on a user’s computer hard drive, which enable a website to “personalize” itself for each user.

Amendments to this Privacy Policy

For information on how we collect, store, share or otherwise use your personal information, see our privacy policy. This statistical data provides us with information about the use of the Website, such as how many visitors visit a specific page on our Website, how long they stay on that page, whether they complete applications or information fields on a web page, which product pictures or other hyperlinks, if any, they “click” on. We also automatically collect your IP address or other unique identifiers (“Device Identifier”) for the computer, mobile device, technology or other devices (collectively, “Device”) you use to access the Websites. A Device Identifier is a number that is automatically assigned to your Device when you access a website or its servers, and our computers identify your Device by its Device Identifier.

Except as set forth in these Terms of Use, or in terms of use applicable to the specific mobile application that generates location-based information, your location will not be shared with third parties without your specific consent.

We may use third parties to assist us with the collection of such Usage Information and Device Identifiers through the use of cookies, pixels and other tracking technologies.

Cookies are data files placed on a Device when it is used to visit the Website. The web analytics provider that we may use to help manage our Website and communications, or our third-party advertising and/or security service providers, may place cookies or similar files on your Device for security and authentication purposes, to deliver our ads, to measure the effectiveness of our advertising, to learn how our users use our Website, to facilitate site navigation and to personalize your experience while visiting our Website. e-Verify
Refundo is an Intermediate Service Provider and uses proprietary technology to facilitate EROs access to their IRS e-Services account with their permission. During enrollment or anytime thereafter you may be asked to provide Refundo your e-Services username, password, and PIN to access your e-Services account and gather information about your firm. Refundo does not store your username, password, or PIN in any format whatsoever. Refundo does not share the information contained inside of your IRS e-Services account and solely use this information for verifying the identity of an ERO.

Refundo, at its sole discretion, may turn away ERO’s based on the information gathered from ERO’s IRS e-Services account.

Refundo’s e-Verify software does not add, delete or modify any information stored on the IRS e-Services system.
